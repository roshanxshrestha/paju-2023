about us

Welcome To Paju Lounge & Family Restaurant

Centrally located in Dhading Bensi, Paju Lounge is what you’d come to expect from classy and fancy restaurants. The charming and rustic decor of Paju with elegant modern facilities is one of the leading lounges in Dhading to chill with friends and family. We feel happy to offer you quality food varieties, all freshly prepared in our own kitchen to enjoy with the peaceful musical environment.
